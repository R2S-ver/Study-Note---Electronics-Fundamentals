Personal studylogbook of electronic fundamentals

Welcome to my learning repository! This project documents my self-study journey in the world of electricity and hardware. I am starting from the very basics of electrical safety and slowly diving into component-level circuit design. Currently, this repository focuses on hardware fundamentals and analog electronics, without involving microcontrollers (like Arduino) or digital logic programming yet.

To better internalize these concepts and understand their practical applications, I have included real-world examples and case studies for each component. This “theory + application” approach helps me review more effectively and bridges the gap between textbook knowledge and actual engineering practice.

The images used in this repository are for educational purposes only. If you are the copyright owner of any image used here and would like to be removed, please contact me.
